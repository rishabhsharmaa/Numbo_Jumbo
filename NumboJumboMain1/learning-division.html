Learn Division Magic!

Hey kids! Do you know what division means? It's when you **share or split** something into equal parts!

Let’s Start with a Simple Example:

Imagine you have 6 yummy apples, and you want to share them with 2 friends. How many apples will each friend get?

6 ÷ 2 = 3! Each friend gets 3 apples.

Another Fun Example:

What if you have 12 oranges, and you want to divide them equally among 4 friends? How many oranges does each friend get?

12 ÷ 4 = 3! Isn't that fair?
