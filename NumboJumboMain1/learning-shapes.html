Learn About Shapes & Patterns!

Hey kids! Did you know that shapes are all around us? Let’s learn about some common shapes and how they fit into patterns!

Here are Some Simple Shapes:

  • Square: A square has 4 equal sides. Look! It’s like a box! 🟦
  • Rectangle: A rectangle has 4 sides, but two sides are longer. It looks like a door! 🟧
  • Triangle: A triangle has 3 sides. It’s like a slice of pizza! 🍕
  • Star: A star has 5 points. It shines brightly in the sky! ⭐
  • Pentagon: A pentagon has 5 sides. It looks like a house with a roof! ⬟
